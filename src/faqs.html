Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 15-day return policy on all items except Firearms, Optics, Knives, Ammunition, and Custom Alterations.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary based on location. The only exception is that firearms must be shiped domestically to a valid Federal Firearms License (FFL) holder.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at customersupport@nelsontactical.com or call us at (920) 884-2580.

Do you sell used guns?

Yes, however in order to give a fair price we ask that you bring your firearm in so we can appraise it to the best of our ability.
